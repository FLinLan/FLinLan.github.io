As a Teaching Assistant for EE280, I facilitate student learning through interactive quiz sections, hands-on laboratory experiences, and individualized academic support. My responsibilities include conducting weekly review sessions that reinforce core concepts, leading practical laboratory exercises, providing detailed feedback on assignments, and maintaining office hours to address student questions.
