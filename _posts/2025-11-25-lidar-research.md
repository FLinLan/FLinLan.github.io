## Project Overview

Conducted research on embedded applications and components of a LIDAR system at the University of Washington Laboratory of Photonic Devices. Implemented serial communication interfaces between microcontrollers and FPGAs for real-time data acquisition and processing.

## Research Focus

- **LIDAR Components:** Photonic device characterization and integration
- **Communication Interfaces:** Serial protocols (UART, SPI) for microcontroller-FPGA communication
- **Embedded Systems:** Real-time data processing and sensor integration
- **Signal Processing:** LIDAR signal acquisition and processing

## Key Accomplishments

- Conducted comprehensive research on LIDAR system components and embedded applications
- Implemented serial communication interfaces (UART, SPI) between microcontrollers and FPGAs
- Developed embedded firmware for real-time LIDAR data acquisition
- Integrated photonic devices with embedded systems for practical applications
- Demonstrated proficiency in hardware-software integration for sensor systems
- Contributed to photonic device research and characterization in laboratory environment
